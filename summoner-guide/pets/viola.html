Viola

Viola has an interesting gimmick in the form of Viola Change, which toggles Viola's other PAs between agile, and slower but harder hitting ones. Unfortunately, Viola remains a pretty niche pick that's out damaged by Synchro and Rappy in most cases.

Viola's rare use comes from the large hitboxes on some of its PAs - if two bosses are near each other (but not close enough for Synchro Geki to hit both, or if Synchro is just too squishy to live in that situation), Viola Pulsar and Viola Stamp can effectively hit both enemies.

Viola Stamp also has a pretty good vertical hitbox - if you're in a situation where a few bosses are stacked sorta, vertically I guess (there was one Limited Quest that had Appregina floating above a Tranmizer), then Viola can be effective.

Viola Change (1 PP)
Changes Viola between its two forms - its default, and a second form, Change Form, with enlarged wings. During the change animation, Viola is invincible.

In this second form, Viola has 40% more DEF (not including DEF gained from Candy), 70% less PP regeneration, and stronger/slower PAs.

Since Viola's winged form offers you increased DPS across the board, you'll rarely want to spend time in the default form outside of recovering PP.

You can also jump during Viola change, and you'll be able to input the next PA 5 frames sooner.



Viola Assault (10 PP)
Viola dashes in a line, then returns your side.



Viola Slicer (25 PP)
Perform two slashes that hit enemies in front of Viola.



Viola Stamp / Stomp (10 PP)
Viola stomps the ground inflicting damage twice. Stamp during Change Form is Viola’s main DPS PA.

Power value of this PA is 70% of the PA's power value for the first hit, then 30% for the shockwave which has longer range. First hit stuns any enemies hit.

During Change form, this changes to 160% of the PA's power value for the first hit, and 160% for the second hit.



Viola Pulsar / Pulser (30 PP)
Viola dashes upwards inflicting a DoT which lasts 14s. Note: A Viola Pulsar casted in Change form counts as a separate DoT. Pulsar in Change form lasts 15s.

While you can stack the DoT from this PA, it's generally not effective DPS to do so.




Change > Pulsar > Stamp x8

8 Viola Stamps fits perfectly into the 15s duration of Change form Pulsar. If you have a lower max PP, you can perform less Stamps instead.




Pulsar > Change > Pulsar > Normal Attack to full PP

Stacking Viola's DoTs before using Viola's normal attacks allows you to deal decent damage while recovering your PP - this is the only time it's worth it to stack Viola's DoTs.

Make sure to leave yourself 61 PP after your damage combo so you have enough PP to stack Pulsars before recovering PP.

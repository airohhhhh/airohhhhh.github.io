Rappy

Rappy is a bossing pet that touts extremely high defense (and 7777 Dexterity at Lv 130), at the cost of noticeably lower damage.

Because of the big damage hit you take by using Rappy instead of Synchro, Rappy should be considered a last choice of sorts if you absolutely can't keep Synchros alive in a boss fight.

Rappy's second use is for it's personality, Early Bird, which reduces the revive time of your other pets while Rappy is equipped; this pairs great with a palette full of Marons in content that builds Marons extremely quickly.

Rappy Drop (20 PP)
Rappy slams into the target dealing damage in an area.

Very high DPS when spammed in conjunction with Rykros Staff, but depletes your PP quickly; this isn't practical for extended use!



Rappy Cannon (15 PP)
Rappy launches a rappy barrel which deals damage if you’re locked onto an enemy. Holding down the PA will launch multiple barrels consecutively. Once Rappy has stopped launching barrels, it returns to your side.

Alongside it's obvious use as a recall PA, the hold function of this PA is actually a pretty good way to hit mobs that are flying around and being obnoxious.



Rappy End / Finisher (40 PP)
Press and hold to summon a tower of rappies and release to deal damage. Charges in 2.5s.

Works well as a pre-charge PA that requires less charge time than Torim/Aero Spiral.

Also, this PA has quite good DPS - if you're not in Alter Ego, where Rykros makes Rappy Sonic your PA of choice, End is an effective DPS tool provided the enemy sits still enough.


Rappy Sonic (20 PP)
Rappy strikes six times. Only activates if Rappy is locked onto a target.

Pairs nearly as well with Rykros as Drop, but with greater PP efficiency. Also note that you can normal attack during this PAs animation, which will result in Sonic tracking the enemy if it moves a bit.

With a decent craft of Concentrated Zanverse, you can fully charge and cast Zanverse during the cast animation of Sonic. If you're running a quest where you're using Rappy, and there's no Techer or any other Zanverse caster around, may as well do some extra damage.


Rappy Melody (20 PP)
Rappy sings adorably (Kyu, Kyu!), damages enemies, and heals itself and allies in the area.

This PA's damage isn't particularly impressive, so you really only want to use this if Rappy's low on health.

Note that you can spam this PA to stack up healing ticks - if Rappy is low on HP, button mash for a bit.

Cery/Popple

While Cery and Popple are adorable, they're garbage at pretty much everything. Cery-ously, never use this pet.

Their mobbing damage is outdone by Jinga and Redran, their bossing damage is nothing to write home about, and its PP Recovery is worse than that of Glass or Viola.

Basic Box
If your goal is just to get your weekly Critical Candy Stick from Pietro, you don't even need to worry about slotting candy on this pet, but if you want to mess around with Popple or make it more powerful, you can use the following layout:

Candy layout that caps on crit with 205 PP or more.

Pancakes: Two Layer Pancake, Three Layer Pancake
Parfaits: Exquisite Deadly Parfait, Super Deadly Parfait
Rolls: Steadfast Roll, Support Roll
Cookies: 3x Tech Cookie
Sticks: 1x Vin-Spi Candy Stick, 2x Critical Candy Stick
Sandwiches: 1x Body Sandwich
Gummies: 3x Stamina Gummy



Upgraded Box
Upgraded box that cuts the Support Roll, Critical Candy Sticks and Tech Cookies for more powerful options. Requires S6: Wise Skill and 250 PP or more to reach 100% crit.

Pancakes: Two Layer Pancake, Three Layer Pancake
Parfaits: Exquisite Deadly Parfait, Super Deadly Parfait
Rolls: Steadfast Roll, Fluffy Roll
Cookies: 3x Tech-Spi Cookie
Sticks: 3x Vin-Spi Candy Stick
Sandwiches: 1x Body Sandwich
Gummies: 3x Stamina Gummy

Once you have 300+ PP, you can replace Exquisite Deadly Parfait with Exquisite Tactics Parfait, and replace the Steadfast Roll with a Bravado Roll.

Popple Splode (20 PP)
Launch a projectile that deals damage in an area.

Can be charged for 1.9s to increase its damage and range.

Also, Splode's projectile moves quicker the further you are from your target. Cursor aim mode is useful for this.

Finally, while Splode can be charged for increased damage and range, this is actually worse for your DPS. In an extended fight, only charge Splode if the increased range is valuable to you.


Popple Fall / Vortex (10 PP)
Pulls enemies together in a small area dealing minor damage.



Popple Territory (15 PP)
Press and hold to continually deal damage in an area.

Drains PP over time and ramps up in damage over time.



Popple Chaser (15 PP)
Popple launches a homing bullet that bounces to other enemies. Knocks enemies down.



Popple Curse (5 PP)
Popple launches a slow moving projectile that inflicts Jellen.

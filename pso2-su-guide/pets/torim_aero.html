Torim/Aero

This pet's main use lies in dealing preemptive damage - use Shout + charged Spiral prior to an enemy spawning, and you can strike it for massive damage right as it spawns.

Spiral's high damage is also an effective DPS tool against bosses that don't have a 999,999 damage cap.

Torim vs. Aero

Torim & Aero have the same PAs and personality, but vary otherwise.

Aero Spiral features a longer-range shockwave that hits if you miss Spiral. Shockwave deals 70% of the damage that Spiral would have. This is useful against mobile enemies, or for landing Spiral from further distances.

Meanwhile, Torim has jacked up HP/DEF, which can make it less risky to charge Spiral against particularly hard-hitting opponents.

Diligent / Industrious

Increases Torim or Aero's damage by 15%, but increases the PP cost of their PAs by 30%.

Torim and Aero's preferred base personality.
Docile / Smart Cookie

Enables Torim or Aero to deal 21% increased damage to enemies currently suffering from a status effect.

Given that this pet is mainly useful against bosses, which can't suffer from status effects (and this pet has no way to inflict status anyways), this personality doesn't really offer much.

Elite ()

Torim or Aero gains the effects of Diligent and Docile alongside +5% RDR.

Torim and Aero's finalized personality.

Aero Shout (26 PP)
Engulfs Aero in wind, dealing minor damage in a small area. Increases the damage of Bullet, Slash, and Spiral for 20s.

Provides a 1.67x increase to the DoT of Bullet, a 1.2x increase to Slash, and a 2.5x increase to charged Spiral.

Always make sure to have this buff up while using Aero.



Aero Spiral (39 PP)
Aero charges up and releases a high damage hit.

Spiral has three variants - uncharged, charged for 2s or more, and charged for 5s. The uncharged version of this PA is never worth your time.



Aero Bullet / Barrage (7 PP)
Aero fires a projectile, then returns to your side. Projectile inflicts a DoT that lasts 11 seconds.

Solid DPS if you intend to have Aero out for extended periods of time. Note that Bullet and Shout-buffed Bullet can stack, as they count as separate attacks.



Aero Slash (20 PP)
Aero performs five quick slashes.

Filler PA if you don't think you have time to fit in a 2s or 5s Spiral.



Aero Stab / Slam (20 PP)
Aero dashes behind an enemy striking them once.

Good for chasing an enemy across the screen and not much else.

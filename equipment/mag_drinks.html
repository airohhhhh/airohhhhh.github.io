Mag and Drinks

Your Mag is a companion that provides you with extra offensive stats and a variety of other actions they can perform in combat that deal damage, heal you, or provide PP recovery.

You'll character gains access to their Mag at level 20 through a Koffie client order, which you can then equip through the Items > Mag screen.

Drinks are temporary buffs that you can pick up before each quest.

Photon Blast, Support Level, Auto Action, and SP Auto Action devices can be obtained at the Photon Sphere and Excube shops.
Photon Blast
The two Photon Blasts worth using are Julius Nykta and Ketos Proi. Julius Nykta can be activated to group an upcoming crowd together for easy mobbing, while Ketos Proi can be used as an additional PP recovery option.

Support Level
As Su/Ph, you'll want a mag with either Dexterity or Technique fully leveled. Both provide the same amount of damage, but Dex slightly lowers the damage you take, making it the slightly better option.

If you're playing Global using Pet Switch Strike on Su/Fi, go for Striking instead.

Auto Action
Megid is your auto action of choice simply because it deals more damage than other auto actions. Be mindful of this against Anga, though, as this can get Dark resisted which guts Rykros damage.

SP Action
Vulcun/Rapid Fire is definitely the best SP action for Summoner. Upon reaching a boss-type enemy, Vulcun will begin firing a string of bullets (90 total), each restoring 1 PP, even during Photon Arts.

Note that Clobber/Dance doesn't increase pet damage.

Trigger Actions
Your preferred trigger actions are going to be PP restore actions, since Summoner doesn't typically get much value from defensive actions.



Your mag will only perform actions if it has the energy to do so - once your Mag is max level, feed it junk (such as Monomates) to keep it's energy up.
PP Restore E + PP Restore J
These two Trigger Actions are useful on Summoner for the High PP recovery they provide. PP Restore E greatly increases your PP recovery for a short while when you fill your Photon Blast guage, while PP Restore E greatly increases your PP recovery for a short while every so often.

While they're desirable for Summoner, they can only be obtained by leveling a fresh mag in a pretty specific way. As such, this is a helpful, but ultimately entirely optional aspect of Summoner gearing.

If you wish to build a Mag with both of these Trigger Actions, start with a level zero Mag and do the following:

  • Raise your Mag's T-ATK stat to 30
  • Then, raise your Mag's Dex stat to 34
  • Finally, raise your Mag's R-ATK stat to 36
  • To complete your mag, fully level your desired stat and spend Excubes to remove unwanted stats.
If performed correctly, the result will be a level 200 Mag with PP Restore E and J.
While there's a wide variety of drinks available, it basically boils down to using Shifta Drink EX / Shifta Drink Premium 2 for every quest.

In a mobbing context, it offers you more damage than the PA Damage increase of Photon Drink EX.

The 50% increase to your base attack offered by this drink also means that your Rykros projectiles deal more damage than when using Prem Weak Point Drink, cementing Shifta Drink as the best choice for bossing as well.


Drinks last for an hour, so you typically won't need to return to campship to rebuff.

Units

Your character can equip three units at once - a Rear, Arm, and Leg piece.

Units provide you with defensive stats, such as HP and DEF, but also with offensive stats like ATK and PP. Because of Dear Master, we don't actually care about defensive stats at all, so all that matters is the ATK and PP values when deciding what units to use.

Supports S8
Units feature SSA slots! Rears feature an S6 SSA slot, Arm units feature an S7 SSA slot, and Leg units feature an S8 SSA slot.

SSAs are typically much more powerful than normal affixes, so you should strive to fill your SSA slots once you've completed all other aspects of Summoner gearing.

Units and Unit SSAs aren't yet available on Global.


Noteworthy S6s:

Wise Skill - Increase critical hit rate by 15%. By gaining critical hit rate from your S6, you’ll be able to replace some of your Support Rolls with defensive rolls which is a huge help in keeping your pets alive.

Brilliant Ruin - Recover 5 PP when an enemy near you is defeated. Provides you with a ton of extra PP recovery in mobbing content.

T-ATK Up - Increase T-ATK by 30. Unit SSAs which grant ATK count as base attack, which means their damage is increased by buffs like Team Attack and Shifta. This makes T-ATK Up a solid choice if you haven’t dropped better S6s.


Noteworthy S7s:

Glowing Grace - Recover 4 PP per second while standing still for longer than 1 second. This is insane on Summoner. As you’re immune to displacements while in Alter Ego because of Alter Ego Harmony, you can frequently choose to stand still and get the effect of this SSA. Glowing Grace makes it really hard to run out of PP on Jinga, and enables you to keep Synchro comboing for ages. Definitely the best Summoner S7 by far.

If you have Glowing Grace equipped, consider making heavier use of Ilzonde. If you cast Ilzonde while on the ground, you'll remain on the ground for the entire technique's duration - so for repositioning short distances, Ilzonde can allow you to quickly move and instantly have Glowing Grace activate again once you halt.

Also, for Jinga Slicer (which can be jump canceled to save PP), it's better to avoid jumping if using Glowing Grace.

T-ATK Up 2 - Increase T-ATK by 50. With damage buffs applied, this SSA represents a notable damage increase. This is a better choice than Glowing Grace in content where you’re constantly moving, (such as tower defense quests), or when using Redran (locking onto enemies with Redran Laser will almost always prevent Glowing Grace from being active).

Decried Warrior - Increase damage by 1%. When an enemy is targeting you, reduce damage taken from it by 15%. Surprisingly, the damage reduction portion of this SSA actually works with pets. Sega did right by Summoner for once.

The +1% damage however has some weird properties. Your pet will gain the +1% regardless of whether it has aggro, but only while the pet near the enemy. Once enemies are targeting you though, your pet will always have the +1% damage, regardless of range.

Because of this inconsistency in whether the damage is active or not, T-ATK Up 2 is still the best choice for multiplayer content where you can’t make use of Glowing Grace, leaving Decried Warrior to be used primarily in solo content.

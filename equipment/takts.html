Takts

Summoner's only weapon type. On top of enabling you to equip pets, Takts also provide other combat bonuses through their potentials.

  • Core Takts
  • Secondary Takts
  • Other Takts
  • Recommended EN Harmonizers


Rykros Staff - (Glance of Darkness)
During Alter Ego, Rykros will automatically activate Pet Sympathy, and fire a single-target dark projectile during each pet PA cast.

Projectiles scale only from T-ATK, and don't fire with Maron or Melon Strike. Upgradable via Ultimate Boosters! Further increases the damage of Rykros projectiles.
This Takt is best used alongside pets with quick PAs when fighting a boss or a small crowd (1-3 mobs), and should only be used during Alter Ego.

The pet that best showcases this is Synchro, but Rykros is also useful on Vulcan, Jinga and Rappy.


Darkbanisher Ayer Reed - (Cycle of Fate)
Increase power by 11%, decrease PP consumption by 20%, and restore 20 PP every 10s.
This weapon is basically Liberate Takt + Milliorazi Orb. Darkbanisher's high power makes this the best Takt for burst (Maron Strike, Aero Spiral), pets that make poor use of Rykros (Melon, Viola, Redran), and a great choice for Rykros pets when Alter Ego is on cooldown (Synchro, Jinga, Rappy, Vulcan).

Also, +20 PP/10s alongside -20% PP cost is massive. It only takes two runs of Primordial Darkness to get the materials needed to build this, so there’s no excuse not to pick one up.




Lightweaver Cras Reed - (Lightsong Guardian)
Grants the same effects as ‘Cycle of Fate’. After manually normal attacking three times, for 30s, change this potential to: Increase power by 15%, reduce damage taken by 30%, and gain Super Armour.

The upgraded form of Darkbanisher that requires you clear Primordial Darkness 50 times.

In most cases, losing Dark Banisher’s PP reduction and PP restoration is detrimental to you, so it’s useful to have both a Darkbanisher and Lightweaver in your inventory - so that you have the option of recovering PP with normals without going to Lightweaver’s effect if desired.

That said, the activated potential of Lightweaver is still of value too. The 15% power allows you to double down on your burst damage at the cost of PP efficiency (+15% Maron Strikes), the 30% damage reduction may allow you to keep your pets alive outside of the safety of Alter Ego, and the Super Armour granted by this allows you to prevent knockback outside of Alter Ego in knockback heavy content.



Lightstream Canxion - (Lightstream Melody)
Increase power by 5%. Additionally, fully restore your PP when casting a PA or Technique while below 10% PP, and gain 3% power for 30s. This effect has a 120s cooldown.

Upgradable with Luminmech Ether Fragments for an extra 3% damage. Activating Darkblast while this weapon is equipped will increase Darkblast's duration by 10s.
Lightstream is useful as a second Takt on pets that burn PP hard, namely Synchro and Redran, or in any situation where you find yourself out of PP. An extremely useful utility weapon.



Atlas Tahat Ex - (Eternal War Scherzo)
Increase your pet’s power by 6%. Once per quest, you can negate a fatal blow to a pet, leaving it at 1 HP. Restores pet HP at regular intervals afterwards.

Does not prevent a fatal blow on Maron or Melon Strike. Power can be increased to 8% via Luminmech Ether Fragments.
This isn't something you should include on your weapon palette all the time, but this can sometimes be useful for keeping your pet alive during a particularly dangerous fight.



Ceres Cicopus Zara - (Immortal Moment)
Gain a power bonus that increases every two hits (6% power at 10 hits). When power bonus is maxed, gain a barrier that grants super armour and 10% damage reduction.

Barrier will fade if you don't land hits on enemies for 15s.
With its 6% power now outdone by multiple other weapons, Zara remains useful only for the super armour it provides.

The main use of Zara's super armour is to prevent you being staggered out of Melon Strike outside of Alter Ego Harmony, but this Takt can be useful in other situations as well.



Liberate Takt - (Liberated Ensemble)
Increase critical hit rate by 20%, increase power by 8%, and decrease PP consumption by 20%.
Can be upgraded via Ether Fragments to increase power from 8 to 11%.
This weapon offers you less PP than Darkbanisher Ayer Reed while being more obnoxious to build - for the most part, there's little reason to use this.

As of Vulcan's introduction to the game, this Takt does have some use.

During Alter Ego, Rykros is Vulcan's Takt of choice, but out of Alter Ego, Liberate Takt offers the same 11% as Ayer while also patching up Vulcan's crit rate.



Milliorazi Orb/Dusk - (Crimson Evening)
Grants 6% power and automatically restores 2 PP every second.

This weapon is no longer of use, since Darkbanisher Ayer Reed offers what this weapon does and much much more.



Aqua Ripple - (Southern Seas)
Increases power by 6%. When your pet's attribute matches the enemy's weak attribute, it heals for a percentage of the damage it deals, up to 30 HP per hit.

This Takt is pretty forgettable. The pet that most likes the idea of extra healing is Synchro, but it would just be a massive damage loss to not use Rykros Staff.

On any other pet that you might want healing on, Aqua Ripple is worse than using Light & Dark Parfait, Fire & Ice Parfait, or Wind & Lightning Parfait, which heal against two attributes, and for 50 HP per hit.



Ban Stil - (Steadfast Solo)
Increase power by 10%. Power bonus decreases by 1% when you take damage (to a minimum of 6%), and recovers by 0.4% every second. When power bonus is at 10%, pet regenerates 10% HP every 10s.

This Takt is really bad. Makes for a very pretty form change though!
Because of Alter Ego Harmony, you take damage when your pet gets hit during Alter Ego. So if your pet gets hit a few times, this weapon becomes a 6% damage bonus with no other merits.

Also, even at max power, this Takt is useless. You'd rather run Dark Banisher, which offers you a power bonus that doesn't disppear when you take hits, and huge PP bonuses.



Rykros Staff - (Sinister Stare)
During Alter Ego, Rykros will automatically activate Pet Sympathy, and fire a single-target dark projectile during each pet PA cast.

Projectiles scale only from T-ATK, and don't fire with Maron or Melon Strike. Upgradable via Ultimate Boosters! Further increases the damage of Rykros projectiles.
The damage provided by this Harmonizer's potential is insane - far and above that of other Harmonizers. Generally, you'll want to use Rykros during Alter Ego, and swap to another Harmonizer when Ego is on cooldown.



Trailblazer Note Canxion - (Photon Resurrection)
Increase power by 5%. Additionally, fully restore your PP when casting a PA or Technique while below 10% PP, and gain 3% power for 30s. This effect has a 120s cooldown.

Upgradable with Luminmech Ether Fragments for an extra 3% damage. Activating Darkblast while this weapon is equipped will increase Darkblast's duration by 10s.
Trailblazer is useful as a second Takt on pets that burn PP hard, namely Synchro and Redran, or in any situation where you find yourself out of PP.

This Harmonizer enables you to fully recover your PP without having to stop dealing damage to normal attack - which really improves your uptime and your consistent damage output. This weapon remains an extremely useful utility weapon for Summoner even on present day JP! Don't skip out on this Harmonizer.



Guren no Yomimusubi - (Melody of Crimson Flame)
Increase damage by 2%. Increases damage by an additional 3% when you match the enemy's weak element. Also casts Shifta on you and your pet periodically.

Guren's Shifta ticks can respond to Pet Shifta Sympathy requests.
Guren is an alright choice on pets like Aero, Maron, and Redran if you don't have access to Trailblazer.

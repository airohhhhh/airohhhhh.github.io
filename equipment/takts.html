Takts

Summoner's only weapon type. On top of enabling you to equip pets, Takts also provide other combat bonuses through their potentials.

  • Core Takts
  • Secondary Takts
  • Other Takts


Rykros Staff 14★ - Glance of Darkness - During Alter Ego, Rykros will automatically activate Pet Sympathy, and fire a single-target dark projectile during each pet PA cast.

Projectiles scale only from T-ATK, and don't fire with Maron or Melon Strike. Upgradable via Ultimate Boosters! Further increases the damage of Rykros projectiles.
This Takt is best used alongside pets with quick PAs when fighting a boss or a small crowd (1-3 mobs), and should only be used during Alter Ego.

The pet that best showcases this is Synchro, but Rykros is also useful on Jinga and Rappy.


Darkbanisher Ayer Reed 15★ - Cycle of Fate - Increase power by 11%, decrease PP consumption by 20%, and restore 20 PP every 10s.
his weapon is basically Liberate Takt + Milliorazi Orb. This weapon’s high power makes this the best Takt for burst (Maron Strike, Aero Spiral), pets that make poor use of Rykros (Melon, Viola, Redran), and a great choice for Rykros pets when Alter Ego is on cooldown (Synchro, Jinga).

Also, +20 PP/10s alongside -20% pp cost is massive. It only takes two runs of Primordial Darkness to get the materials needed to build this, so there’s no excuse not to pick one up.




Lightstream Canxion 15★ - Lightstream Melody - Increase power by 5%. Additionally, fully restore your PP when casting a PA or Technique while below 10% PP, and gain 3% power for 30s. This effect has a 120s cooldown.

Upgradable with Luminmech Ether Fragments for an extra 3% damage.
Lightstream is useful as a second Takt on pets that burn PP hard, namely Synchro and Redran, or in any situation where you find yourself out of PP.



Atlas Tahat Ex 15★ - Eternal War Scherzo - Increase your pet’s power by 6%. Once per quest, you can negate a fatal blow to a pet, leaving it at 1 HP. Restores pet HP at regular intervals afterwards.

Does not prevent a fatal blow on Maron or Melon Strike. Power can be increased to 8% via Luminmech Ether Fragments.
This isn't something you should include on your weapon palette all the time, but this can sometimes be useful for keeping your pet alive during a particularly dangerous fight.



Ceres Cicopus Zara 14★ - Immortal Moment - Gain a power bonus that increases every two hits (6% power at 10 hits). When power bonus is maxed, gain a barrier that grants super armour and 10% damage reduction.

Barrier will fade if you don't land hits on enemies for 15s.
With its 6% power now outdone by multiple other weapons, Zara remains useful only for the super armour it provides.

The main use of Zara's super armour is to prevent you being staggered out of Melon Strike outside of Alter Ego Harmony, but this Takt can be useful in other situations as well.



Lightweaver Cras Reed 15★ - Lightsong Guardian - Grants the same effects as ‘Cycle of Fate’. After using 3 normal attacks, for 30s, change this potential to: Increase power by 15%, reduce damage taken by 30%, and gain Super Armour.

The upgraded form of Darkbanisher that requires you clear Primordial Darkness 50 times.

In most cases, losing Dark Banisher’s PP reduction and PP restoration is detrimental to you, so it’s useful to have both a Darkbanisher and Lightweaver in your inventory - so that you have the option of recovering PP with normals without going to Lightweaver’s effect if desired.

That said, the +15% power and 30% damage reduction of Lightweaver's potential is still of value too - the 15% power allows you to double down on your burst damage, at the cost of PP efficiency, while the 30% damage reduction may allow you to keep your pets alive outside of the safety of Alter Ego.



Milliorazi Orb/Dusk 15★ - Crimson Evening - Grants 6% power and automatically restores 2 PP every second.

This weapon is no longer of use, since Darkbanisher Ayer Reed offers what this weapon does and much much more.



Liberate Takt 15★ - Liberated Ensemble - Increase critical hit rate by 20%, increase power by 8%, and decrease PP consumption by 20%.
This weapon offers you less power and PP than Darkbanisher Ayer Reed while being more obnoxious to build - there's no reason to use this Takt anymore.


Aqua Ripple 15★ - Southern Seas - Increases power by 6%. When your pet's attribute matches the enemy's weak attribute, it heals for a percentage of the damage it deals, up to 30 HP per hit.

This Takt is pretty forgettable. The pet that most likes the idea of extra healing is Synchro, but it would just be a massive damage loss to not use Rykros Staff.

On any other pet that you might want healing on, Aqua Ripple is worse than using Light & Dark Parfait, Fire & Ice Parfait, or Wind & Lightning Parfait, which heal against two attributes, and for 50 HP per hit.



Ban Stil 1★ - Steadfast Solo - Increase power by 10%. Power bonus decreases by 1% when you take damage (to a minimum of 6%), and recovers by 0.4% every second. When power bonus is at 10%, pet regenerates 10% HP every 10s.

This Takt is really bad. Makes for a very pretty form change though!
Because of Alter Ego Harmony, you take damage when your pet gets hit during Alter Ego. So if your pet gets hit a few times, this weapon becomes a 6% damage bonus with no other merits.

Also, even at max power, this Takt is useless. You'd rather run Dark Banisher, which offers you a power bonus that doesn't disppear when you take hits, and huge PP bonuses.
